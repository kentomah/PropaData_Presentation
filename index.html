Agentic AI for Fish Value chain

Autonomous, Specialized Intelligence Agents

What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to autonomous software agents that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals without constant human intervention.

Why Agentic AI for iSOKO?

  • Real-time decision making for perishable goods
  • Automated compliance monitoring
  • Personalized guidance for diverse user needs
  • Scalable intelligence across 7 countries
  • Low-resource operation suitable for rural areas

The Necessity of AI Agents in Fish Value Chain

Fisheries commerce faces unique challenges that make agentic AI essential:

Time Sensitivity
Fish is highly perishable - decisions must be made in real-time. AI agents provide instant recommendations for pricing, routing, and inventory management.
Complex Compliance
Cross-border trade involves multiple regulations. AI agents continuously monitor SPS requirements, documentation, and policy changes across borders.
Multi-Stakeholder Ecosystem
Fisheries involve traders, transporters, regulators, and buyers. AI agents coordinate across stakeholders to optimize the entire value chain.

AI-Driven Capacity Building

Our AI agents don't just provide answers - they identify knowledge gaps and recommend personalized training:

Personalized Learning Paths
When AI detects a trader struggling with SPS compliance or cold chain management, it automatically recommends targeted courses from the Knowledge Hub.
Just-in-Time Learning
As traders prepare for cross-border shipments, the AI provides micro-learning modules on specific requirements for their destination country.
